1. How to fill out a PAR on IPPS-A

Log in to IPPS-A at https://hr.ippsa.army.mil and go to Self-Service > Personnel Actions. Click Create New Request, select the type, fill it out, and submit with supporting docs.

Common issues include missing marriage certificates or wrong document format.
2. How to read your Soldier Talent Profile on IPPS-A

Access via Self-Service > My Profile or directly at Soldier Talent Profile. Review sections for education, assignments, performance, and personal data.

3. How to find certificates in IPERMS

From IPPS-A dashboard, click IPERMS. Go to "My Record" > "Training/Education". Or access directly: iperms.hrc.army.mil.

4. How to read your Leave Earnings Statement (LES)

Log into myPay and select LES. Review entitlements, deductions, leave balance, and net pay.

6. Accessing Army Publications

Go to Army Publications and search for ARs, DAPAMs, FMs, ATMs, and DA Forms like DA 31.

7. How to check appointments and results on MEDPROS

Log in to MEDPROS via https://medpros.mods.army.mil and select "My Medical Readiness Status". You can see labs, PHA, and dental results.
